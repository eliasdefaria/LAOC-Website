Who We Are

The LA Ocean Coalition is a UCLA based organization dedicated to the preservation of our planet’s oceans. We organize sustainability themed events for those who are interested including beach cleanups and educational events. Through our work promoting sustainability on campus at the University of California, Los Angeles, we hope to make a change for the positive within our community.

We Need Your Help

We can't do it alone. Here are three ways you can support our mission to preserve our planet's oceans.

I. Get Involved

Join us as we try to fight plastics pollution with boots on the ground. Volunteer with us and come to one of our upcoming beach cleanups. If you crave to do more, become a team ambassador and apply for one of our open positions such as Designer or PR Manager to become an LA Ocean Coalition admin.

II. Make a Contribution

You may have heard money makes the world go round. We’re here to tell you, it also keeps our oceans clean. Any funds contributed to our gofundme are put toward preserving our beautiful oceans.

III. Attend our Events

Sign up for our next beach cleanup or take a step further and sign up for our newsletter to keep up to date on our upcoming events like art exhibitions or future beach cleanups.
