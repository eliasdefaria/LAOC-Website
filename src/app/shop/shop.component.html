LA Ocean Coalition shirts made from 100% recycled plastics. The plastics are collected from waste management facilities throughout the eastern United States that are then shredded into pellets. These pellets are then melted down and extruded into yarn that is used to cut and sew the shirts. Learn more about the process and our retailer here.
